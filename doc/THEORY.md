some theory about FDM

Magnetic field contribution from a current element:

$$
d\mathbf{B} = \frac{\mu_0 I}{4\pi} \frac{d\vec{\ell} \times \hat{r}}{r^2}
$$

- `I` — current [A]  
- `R` — loop radius [m]  
- `mu_0` — permeability of free space, 4π × 10⁻⁷ T·m/A  

On-axis field at distance z from loop centre:

$$
B_z(z) = \frac{\mu_0 I R^2}{2(R^2 + z^2)^{3/2}}
$$

At centre (z = 0):

$$
B_0 = \frac{\mu_0 I}{2R}
$$
